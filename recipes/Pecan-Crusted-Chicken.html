Pecan Crusted Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Pecans
  • Salt/Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Fresh Rosemary (optional)

How to make pecan crusted chicken

  1. Pulse pecans, herbs, and spices in a food processor.
  2. Assemble chicken breasts, pecan mixture, and eggs.
  3. Dip your chicken breasts in your egg mixture.
  4. Immediately dip in pecan mixture. You may have to press down to fully coat it.
  5. Lay coated chicken on a parchment lined baking sheet.
  6. Bake until brown and crispy.
