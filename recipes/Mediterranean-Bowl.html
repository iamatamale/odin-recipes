Mediterranean Bowl

Ingredient List

  • Hummus
  • Chickpeas
  • Cucumber
  • Green and/or Black olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Couscous
  • Tomatoes
  • Red Onion
  • Spinach
  • Olive oil
  • Other toppings

Instructions

  1. Make the hummus: Add the chickpeas to a blender and blend until powder. Add other ingredients and blend until smooth.
  2. Assemble the bowls: Add the cooked couscous and baby spinach leaves to the bowls.
  3. Spoon the hummus into the bowl and add the other salad ingredients. Drizzle with olive oil, freshly cracked black pepper and any fresh herbs you have and serve.

Tips for making hummus bowls

  1. Assemble the salads just before serving. This way the hummus doesn't add too much moisture to the other fresh ingredients.
  2. Don't add any dressing.The hummus will act like a thick dressing, a splash of olive oil is recommended though.
  3. Cut the salad ingredients into similar sized pieces. This will make it easier and more consistant to eat.
